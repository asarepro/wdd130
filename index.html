Ghana

Ghana is a West African nation known for its diverse landscape, rich culture,
and historic role as the first independent sub-Saharan African country.
Located on the Gulf of Guinea, Ghana's geography varies from coastal savannas and rainforests to inland plains.
The country boasts a diverse population of over 100 ethnic groups,
each with its own traditions and customs.
